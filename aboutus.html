About Us


Scott Tapp and his staff have worked on the sets of Hollywood to some of the states largest high rises. After many years of working with some of the cities largest contractors, Scott took a leap and opened his own contracting company. Operating in the Austin, TX area, TappCo Construction & Remodeling is there for any construction, remodeling, or renovation project desired.

That leap has generated a company with a loyal customer base that request him to complete large scale high-end remodeliing jobs to emergency repairs. You have an idea, TappCo Construction will make it a reality. Everything from small repairs to full home renovations, TappCo Construction will take care of the project.

Instilled with a work ethic that is seldom seen today, Scott works toward his clients goals and aspirations. Learning from his previous employers and finding his own style, Scott has completed many projects throught the years. Scott also helps to educate and inspire others toward this trade business.
